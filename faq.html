Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about joining India's most exclusive network of venture capitalists and unicorn founders.

Membership

Who can join Beeda?

Beeda is exclusively for founders with $2M+ ARR or Seed+ funding, scaling companies beyond $25M+ ARR, angel investors who are previous founders, and Tier 1 & 2 VCs, LPs, and PE firms deploying significant capital in the Indian ecosystem. All applications are reviewed by our membership committee to ensure the highest quality network.

How do I apply for membership?

You can request an invitation through our website or get a referral from an existing member. The application process includes a review of your background, company traction, and alignment with Beeda's mission. We prioritize quality over quantity, maintaining an exclusive community of India's top entrepreneurs and investors.

What are the membership fees?

Membership details including fees are shared during the application process. We believe in creating value through exclusive access, premium experiences, and meaningful connections rather than volume-based networking. Investment in Beeda membership pays for itself through the opportunities and partnerships formed within our community.

Events & Experiences

What types of events does Beeda host?

We host intimate mansion gatherings, Michelin-star dinners, exclusive retreats, and invite-only strategy sessions across Delhi, Mumbai, and Bengaluru. Our events are designed for meaningful connections over world-class experiences, with typically 25-50 carefully curated attendees per event.

How often are events held?

Beeda hosts 2-3 exclusive events per month across our three key cities. Each event is meticulously planned to ensure the highest quality of networking and experience. Members receive early access to event announcements and priority booking for our most sought-after gatherings.

Access & Benefits

What is the digital community like?

Our private Slack network is where deals happen between events. Members share insights, deal flow, scaling strategies, and collaboration opportunities. The community is active 24/7 with dedicated channels for different sectors, funding stages, and geographic regions.

How does Beeda Connect work?

Every month, our team makes curated introductions between members based on complementary needs, investment interests, and collaboration opportunities. We analyze member profiles, current priorities, and strategic goals to facilitate high-value connections that drive real business outcomes.

What kind of deal flow access do members get?

Members gain access to stealth investment opportunities, private capital pools, and pre-market deal flow from India's top startups. Our network includes founders raising from seed to Series C+, creating opportunities for strategic investments, partnerships, and early access to breakthrough companies before they hit mainstream radar.

Our team is here to help you understand how Beeda can accelerate your journey.
